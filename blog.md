### [Blockchain-Based Agent Communities Will Emerge](/pwm_nonprofit/blog/agent-communities/)

**Position paper.** Why an open, self-coordinating network of specialist AI agents — coordinating and settling value on a blockchain — is the natural equilibrium, argued from [six principles](/pwm_nonprofit/whitepaper/). Includes why the grand hard problems become long-tail specialties where one focused agent beats a generalist.

### [Physics World Model: An Agent Science Blockchain Platform](/pwm_nonprofit/blog/physics-world-model/)

**Platform paper.** PWM as one realization of that community, for science: AI4Science used like Claude Code and Codex with GPU, computational imaging already verified and ahead of general agents, and how to mine PWM and use AI4Science freely.

### [How to Make a Blockchain Productive](/pwm_nonprofit/blog/productive-blockchain/)

**Essay.** Busy is not productive. A blockchain becomes productive when its token is a claim on real work (Principle 5) — and when the act it rewards most is creating new agents: build a new agent, do real work, earn PWM.
