    Each submission goes through S1–S4 mathematical verification. Verified entries go mainnet and earn PWM tokens. Start from a Principle — or jump to any layer.

    Principle

    A governing physical law — governing equations, conservation laws, symmetry principles. The foundation every Digital Twin and Benchmark builds on.

    Digital Twin

    A forward operator over domain Ω with tolerance ε. Defines the mathematical interface between a Principle and a testable Benchmark.

    5 fields 10% royalty on L4 events

    L3 ☆

    Benchmark

    A fixed dataset + success metric that defines what "solved" means. Every verified Solution that uses your Benchmark pays you royalties.

    5 fields 15% royalty on L4 events
    Submit Benchmark →

    A verified solver for a Principle. Describe your algorithm or AI output. Mismatch check is embedded in S2 verification — no separate submission needed.
